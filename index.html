Case Overview

Active

CASE SUMMARY — A series of mammal disappearances have been reported across multiple districts of Suntopia. Victims were last seen in the Rainforest District, Tundratown, Downtown, Savanna Central, Canyonlands, and surrounding areas. All missing individuals appear to be predator-type mammals.

CCTV footage recovered from Rainforest District CAM+097 at timestamp 11:29:42 shows suspicious paw print activity and unidentified claw markings on pavement. Two distinct footprint types have been identified at the scene.

Seven persons of interest have been identified and profiled. Vehicle registration plates have been partially redacted pending further authorisation. Audio testimony has been collected from three witnesses — recordings are incomplete and under active analysis.

Paw print samples from all seven suspects have been logged in the Suntopia Paw Print Database for forensic cross-referencing against CCTV evidence.
